Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply navigate to the "Courses" page, find a course you're interested in, and click the "Enroll Now" or "Buy Now" button. You will be guided through the payment process.

How do I become an instructor?

Click the "Register" button on the home page and select the "Instructor" role. After you fill out your information, your account will be submitted for admin approval. You will be notified once it's reviewed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and bank transfers. (Note: Payment processing is not yet implemented).
